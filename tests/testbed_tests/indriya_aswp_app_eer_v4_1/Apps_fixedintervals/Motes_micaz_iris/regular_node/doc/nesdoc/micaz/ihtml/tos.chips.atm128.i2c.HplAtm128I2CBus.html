Interface: tos.chips.atm128.i2c.HplAtm128I2CBus

interface HplAtm128I2CBus

This driver implements direct I2C register access and a blocking master controller for the ATmega128 via a Hardware Platform Layer (HPL) to its two-wire-interface (TWI) hardware subsystem.
